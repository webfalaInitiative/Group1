About Us

At SafeHer Refuge, we are dedicated to providing a safe and supportive space for survivors of Sexual and Gender-Based Violence (SGBV). We are passionate about empowering survivors by offering resources, community support, and a platform for healing and resilience. We stand together with survivors, advocating for their rights and providing a refuge where their voices are heard and their experiences validated.

Join us in our commitment to creating a world free from SGBV, one where survivors are respected, supported, and empowered to thrive.
