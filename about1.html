About Us

At SafeHer Refuge, we are dedicated to providing a safe and supportive space for survivors of Sexual and Gender-Based Violence (SGBV). We are passionate about empowering survivors by offering resources, community support, and a platform for healing and resilience. We stand together with survivors, advocating for their rights and providing a refuge where their voices are heard and their experiences validated.

Join us in our commitment to creating a world free from SGBV, one where survivors are respected, supported, and empowered to thrive.

Our Vision

Our vision at SafeHer Refuge is a world where survivors of sexual and gender-based violence (SGBV) find safety, support, and empowerment in their journey towards healing and justice. We envision a society free from SGBV, where survivors are respected, believed, and empowered to thrive.

Our Mission

Our vision at SafeHer Refuge is a world where survivors of sexual and gender-based violence (SGBV) find safety, support, and empowerment in their journey towards healing and justice. We envision a society free from SGBV, where survivors are respected, believed, and empowered to thrive.

What We Do

Through our wide range of services, including counseling, legal assistance, community support groups, and educational resources, we strive to meet the diverse needs of survivors at every stage of their healing journey. Our approach is survivor-centered, trauma-informed, and rooted in principles of empathy, compassion, and respect..

Our Values

We believe in the power of survivors to heal, grow, and thrive, and we are dedicated to providing them with the tools, resources, and support they need to reclaim their agency and autonomy. We are committed to creating a safe and inclusive environment where survivors feel heard, believed, and supported without judgment or discrimination.
