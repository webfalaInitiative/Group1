Welcome To Our Community

Welcome to the Safeher Refuge community, where survivors of sexual and gender-based violence (SGBV) find strength, support, and solidarity. Joining our community means becoming part of a network of survivors, allies, and advocates dedicated to creating a safer, more compassionate world for all.

check

Want To Join Our Community?

Volunteer to make a difference in the lives of survivors by volunteering your time and skills. Whether you're interested in facilitating support groups, providing administrative assistance, or helping with outreach efforts, there are many ways to contribute to our mission.

Attend Events: and join us at community events, workshops, and fundraisers to connect with fellow survivors, allies and advocates, and learn more about how you can get involved in our mission.

check

Why Join Our Community?

  • We provide access to a supportive community of survivors who understands and validates your experiences, providing a safe space for healing and connection.
  • You will gain access to a wide range of support services, educational resources, and empowerment tools to help you on your journey towards recovery and resilience.
  • Find strength and empowerment through advocacy, activism, and collective action, as we work together to create positive change and prevent future harm.
